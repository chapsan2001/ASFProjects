How to install My First Calculator v1.0 on your smartphone

Step 1. Send an APK file located in the My First Calculator folder to the smartphone.
Also, you can download it from GitHub (https://github.com/chapsan2001/ASFProjects/blob/master/my_first_calculator/MyFirstCalculator.apk).

Step 2. Change safety settings on your smartphone to enable installing apps from unknown sources.

Step 3. Launch the APK file.

Done.
